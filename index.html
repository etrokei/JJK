What is Jujutsu Kaisen: 101, and what it does contain?

Jujutsu Kaisen: 101 is a fan-website made by Aiden Chen, which solely focuses on the manga and anime adaptation, Jujutsu Kaisen, written by Gege Akutami (which you can watch on Crunchyroll, Hulu or Netflix). It will cover Jujutsu Kaisen's plot, storyline, media as well as additional information such as the author's bio and awards. It provides all the necessary information needed for a new fan just getting in to the show or a returning fan wanting a refresher or review on what Jujutsu Kaisen is and what has happened thus far. Note that this is simply a fan-site, so minor inaccuracies or mistakes are prone to happen. Enjoy Jujutsu Kaisen: 101 and click a section in the navigator to get started on your journey in the jujutsu world.
